Highlights
  • Providing Environment for Holistic Development through Curricular, Professional, Co-curricular and Extra Curricular activities
  • Consistent Placement record
  • Choice Based Credit system with more number and variety of electives
  • Provision to choose Job Oriented Electives, Skill based Lab Electives and Open Electives
  • Hands on learning integrated into the Curriculum, provisions for Internships
  • Provision to opt for Minor programme along with regular B.Tech. programme with 20 more additional credits offered by other departments.
    (Ex. B.Tech. Mechanical Engineering with Minor in Computer Science and Engineering)
  • Provision to opt for Honors programme along with regular B.Tech. programme with 20 more additional credits from same discipline
    (Ex. B.Tech.(Honors) in Computer Science & Engineering (with Specialization in Full Stack Web Development))
  • Industry Oriented Lectures/activities from Experts
  • Dedicated center for Innovation and Incubation activities
  • Well planned Placement assistance and training activities
  • In built provision for social activities through Center for Fostering Social Responsibility (CFSR)
  • Provision for participation in Cultural activities through Gayatri College Cultural Club (GC3)
  • Indoor and Outdoor sports facilities
  • Stress free environment with freedom to learn
  • Value added programmers like Hackathons, Expert trainings, workshops through professional clubs
  • Industry Institute Interaction through various MoUs with Industries
