Highlights of M.Tech - VLSI Design & Embedded Systems Program
 

  • Department has 47 faculty members, 18 faculty members are Doctorates in various specializations and 18 faculty members are pursuing Ph.D. in various universities and NITs.

  • More than 200 research papers authored by the faculty of ECE published in Journals including IEEE, IET, Elsevier and Springer.

  • More than 150 research papers authored by the faculty of ECE published in Conferences organized by the reputed Organizations.

  • 30 faculty members completed SWAYAM online courses in 60 various advanced courses

  • M.Tech. Labs are full equipped with modern Software and Hardware(such as HFSS, MATLAB and supporting H/W) for carrying out lab experiments and Project work.

  • M.Tech. students are carrying out their Mini-Projects and Main Projects in various reputed organizations such as NITs, ISRO, DRDO.

  • M.Tech. Students are presenting their Project work in various National and International Conferences organized by IEEE, Springer and other reputed Organizations.

  • More 40 papers were authored by the students of M.Tech.(VDES) in reputed Journals like IEEE, IET, Elsevier and Springer.

  • Workshops and Training Programmes in thrust area are being organized for M.Tech.(VDES) students.

  • Training Programmes for Placement in IT Industries and Core Industries are being conducted for M.Tech.(VDES) students.

  • GATE stipend through MHRD, Government of India (@ Rs.12,400/- per month).

  • Finance support of Rs.5,000/- is provided to GATE students (in addition to financial assistant from MHRD).

  • Financial support is also available under Sahakara Scheme (earn while learn) to other meritorious students who are economically poor.

  • M.Tech. Students are also joining for Ph.D. in Reputed Universities, NITs and IITs.

  • M.Tech. Alumni of ECE are working as Scientist, R&D Engineers and Design Engineers in reputed Organizations like ISRO, DRDO, BHEL, INTEL, TCS, WIPRO and other multi-national Companies.
